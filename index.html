DS 4200 - Final Project: Analyzing Features Contributing to Music Popularity

Data Source Used: Spotify

Project Introduction

Music is an integral part of our lives. It is a medium of showing and feeling expression and understanding emotion which is shared throughout all the demographics of the human population. We as a group enjoy listening to music and we know everyone else does too. We all want to keep up with the trending songs and listen to what others are listening, as if other people liked it there is a good chance you will too. But what makes a song popular? And how do different music streaming apps determine that? We tried to answer these questions by using spotify which is arguably the most widely used platform for music. Through this analysis, we aim to uncover patterns and insights that define what makes a song successful and provide us the basis for discovering songs that could become popular.
